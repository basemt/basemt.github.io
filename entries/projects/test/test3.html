@NYCSLICE IS AN ANONYMOUS INSTAGRAM PAGE THAT HAS BEEN DOCUMENTING THE COST OF A SINGLE SLICE OF PLAIN OR PEPPERONI PIZZA AT DIFFERENT PLACES AROUND THE CITY SINCE 2014.

LOVE IT OR HATE IT, PIZZA HAS BECOME A UBIQUITOUS SYMBOL OF NYC’S FOOD CULTURE. WHETHER IT’S FOR LUNCH OR IN A DRUNKEN HAZE, PIZZA JUST ALWAYS HITS THE SPOT.

THIS WEBSITE IS AN ATTEMPT AT CREATIVELY VISUALIZING THIS PUBLIC DATA SET WHILE OFFERING LOCALS AND TOURISTS ALIKE THE ULTIMATE GUIDE TO PIZZA SLICES AROUND NYC.
